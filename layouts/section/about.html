Here's a little bit about me: I'm a linguistics graduate from the University of Melbourne, and I live and work on Wurundjeri country. You might've gauged some of my academic interests by reading my work, but if not, I'm fascinated by the phonology and semantics of Australian Indigenous languages, as well as speech processing and theories of morphology and the lexicon.

In my daily life: I love to walk and be outside; I listen to lots of jazz and funk music; I am vegetarian and I like to cook; I prefer to write on a manual typewriter or with a fountain pen; I am a satisfied neovim convert; I quite enjoy whisky; I will always advocate for well-typeset documents; my goal is to meditate and be grateful for something every day.
